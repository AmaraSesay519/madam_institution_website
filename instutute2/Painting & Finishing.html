Painting & Finishing

Learn the fundamentals of painting and finishing techniques.

Course Overview

This course covers the basics of painting and finishing, including surface preparation, paint application techniques, and finishing touches. Students will gain hands-on experience with various painting tools and materials.

What You'll Learn

  • Surface preparation techniques
  • Types of paints and finishes
  • Application methods (brush, roller, spray)
  • Color theory and selection
  • Finishing touches and detailing
