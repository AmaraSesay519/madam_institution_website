Plumbing Basics Training

Course Overview

Our Plumbing Basics training program is designed to provide participants with the foundational skills and knowledge necessary for a successful career in plumbing. This course covers various aspects of plumbing systems, installation techniques, safety practices, and maintenance procedures.

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Plumbing Systems
  • Types of Plumbing Materials
  • Tools and Equipment Used in Plumbing
  • Installation Techniques
  • Pipe Fitting and Connections
  • Safety Practices in Plumbing
  • Hands-on Practical Sessions
