Masonry (Block Laying) Training

Course Overview

Our Masonry (Block Laying) training program is designed to provide participants with the fundamental skills and knowledge necessary for a successful career in masonry. This course covers various aspects of block laying, including material selection, construction techniques, safety practices, and quality control.



Course Modules

  • Introduction to Masonry and Block Laying
  • Types of Masonry Materials
  • Tools and Equipment Used in Block Laying
  • Block Laying Techniques
  • Mortar Mixing and Application
  • Safety Practices in Masonry
  • Hands-on Practical Sessions
