Solar Installation

Learn the essentials of solar panel installation and maintenance.

Course Overview

This course provides an introduction to solar panel installation, covering key concepts such as system design, installation techniques, and maintenance procedures. Students will learn how to effectively install and maintain solar energy systems.

What You'll Learn

  • Basics of solar energy and photovoltaic systems
  • System design and component selection
  • Installation techniques and best practices
  • Maintenance and troubleshooting of solar systems
  • Safety protocols for working with solar installations
