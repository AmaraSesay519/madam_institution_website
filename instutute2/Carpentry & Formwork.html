Carpentry & Formwork Training

Course Overview

Our Carpentry & Formwork training program is designed to equip participants with the essential skills and knowledge required for a successful career in the construction industry. This course covers various aspects of carpentry, including formwork design, construction techniques, safety practices, and material handling.

Course Modules

  • Introduction to Carpentry and Formwork
  • Types of Formwork Systems
  • Materials Used in Formwork
  • Formwork Design Principles
  • Construction Techniques
  • Safety Practices in Carpentry
  • Hands-on Practical Sessions
