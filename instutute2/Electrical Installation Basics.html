Electrical Installation Basics

Learn the fundamentals of electrical installation and safety.

Course Overview

This course covers the basics of electrical installation, including wiring, circuit protection, and safety practices. Students will gain hands-on experience with electrical tools and components.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic electrical theory and principles
  • Wiring techniques and connections
  • Circuit protection devices (fuses, breakers)
  • Safety practices in electrical work
  • Installation of basic electrical components
