Taileing

Learn the fundamentals of taileing techniques.

Course Overview

This course covers the basics of taileing, including material selection, mixing techniques, and application methods. Students will gain hands-on experience with various taileing tools and materials.

What You'll Learn

  • Material selection for taileing
  • Mixing techniques for different taileing materials
  • Application methods and tools
  • Surface preparation for taileing
  • Finishing techniques and quality control
