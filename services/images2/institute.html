A Safe & Supportive Learning Environment

We understand the responsibility parents carry when sending their loved ones for training. Our institute provides a safe, disciplined, and supportive environment where students are guided by experienced instructors and mentors.

Our focus is not only on skills, but also on character, responsibility, and work ethics.

About Our Training Institute

Our Construction Skills Training Institute is part of YaqAmrakabSesay Construction. We train young people and adults with practical construction skills to prepare them for real job opportunities in the building industry. build a solid foundation for a successful career in construction through our hands-on courses.

Parents can confidently enroll their loved ones knowing they will receive quality training, discipline, and hands-on experience. after completing the courses, students will be job-ready for various construction roles. and our institute is committed to empowering the next generation of skilled construction workers in Magburaka, Sierra Leone. by providing practical training and mentorship. finally, by the end of the course student will awred a certificate.
